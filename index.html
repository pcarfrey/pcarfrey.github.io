Wedding Agenda

Friday: Wedding Rehersal and Dinner

Meet at the churh by 6:00pm (come as early as 5:30pm). No need to dress up, casual clothes are fine. Afterward, dinner will be provided for you and your significant other. After the wedding rehersal we will give you details on where to meet for dinner and where to park.

Saturday: Wedding

We will meet at the churh at 1:00pm and get ready for the wedding, bring everything you will need for the whole day (all the stuff for your tux etc). The wedding starts at at 2:30pm. After the wedding we will be taking pictures and heading straight to the reception hall. The reception starts at 5:00pm.
